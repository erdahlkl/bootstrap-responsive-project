breakfast

grandma's pancakes

ingreidents:

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 2 teaspoon of vanilla
  • 2/3 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup of milk

instructions

  • 1. Mix all of the ingredients into a bowl and mix with a spoon or whisk
  • 2. Let the batter sit for ~5 minutes
  • 3. Get a large pan, put some butter into the pan, and put on medium heat
  • 4. Place about 1/2 cup to a cup of batter into the pan and cook!

crack it and smack it eggs

ingreidents:

  • 1 bagel (plain or everything bagel recommended)
  • 2 eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt & Pepper

instructions

  • 1. Put the bagel into a toaster / heat up the bagel
  • 2. While the bagel is toasting, crack you egg(s) into a bowl and whisk. Add a little salt into the eggs and stir
  • 3. Start to heat up a pan with medium heat. Spray pan with cooking spray
  • 4. Once the bagel is ready, put your liking of butter onto the bagel and put aside
  • 5. Pour the eggs into the pan and cook to liking
  • 6. Once the eggs are cooked, place them on-top of the bagel
  • 7. Add salt and pepper and eat!
